About us

We have come with the idea of visiting every spots in Japan 3 years ago. In the past few years, we had mainly visited Japan simply because it's near our country.

Osaka 2018

Osaka is a designated city in the Kansai region of Japan. It is well-known for its unique traditional food, especially Takoyaki. April in Osaka was much better than my expectation. It was extraordinary marvellous when you walked on a street surrounded by uncountable cherry blossom trees.

Niseko 2017

Niseko is a town located in Shiribeshi Subprefecture, Hokkaido, Japan. As an experienced snowboarder, I can guarantee that Niseko can definitely get into the world top 10 ski resort. It is extremely big with a variety of skiing difficulties, which means it's suitable for people in all levels.
